📘 Student-Assistant

Student-Assistant is a graduation project designed to provide students with a smart platform that supports their daily academic life.
The system offers a set of digital tools and services that help students organize their schedules, manage tasks, and communicate more effectively with peers and the university.

🎯 Project Objectives

Assist students in managing their academic schedules and tasks.

Provide a unified platform for organization, studying, and communication.

Enhance the overall student experience through smart technological solutions.

🔑 Key Features

Schedule Management:
View and organize lectures, exams, and important deadlines.

Smart Reminders:
Notify students of upcoming classes, project submissions, or assignments.

Digital Library & Resources:
Share and access notes, documents, and study materials with classmates.

Communication Tools:
Messaging or notification system for connecting with instructors and peers.

Performance Tracking:
Monitor grades, assignments, and progress in different courses.

Personal AI Assistant (Optional):
Suggest study plans, answer course-related questions, and support learning.

🏗️ Technical Aspects

Frontend: Flutter / Anguler (Web and Mobile Application).

Backend: Node.js with NestJS.

Database: MongoDB.

Authentication: Secure login with JWT.

Notifications: Real-time updates using Socket.IO or Firebase.

🌍 Target Audience

University and college students.

Professors and academic staff (as supporting users).

🚀 Expected Impact

Reduce time wasted and avoid missing deadlines.

Increase students’ productivity and efficiency.

Improve communication between students and the university.

Provide a scalable and smart platform that can be expanded with more features in the future.
